Services

Consulting, integration, and implementation for agents that stay reliable.

Clear roles, constraints, and outcomes. We add observability and evaluation so teams can trust automation. This page explains how we partner with teams—not just what we build.

What we do

Custom Agent Design

Role discovery workshops, decision trees, and constraint maps. We define success metrics before any code is written.

Integration & Scaling

Data, tools, and workflow integration with observability dashboards and evaluation harnesses to keep agents measurable.

Advisory & Implementation

Build-versus-buy calls, governance patterns, playbooks for rollout, and implementation support from MVP to production.

How clients typically engage

  • Discovery and role definition workshops to align scope and success criteria.
  • Pilots with narrow surfaces and measurable outcomes to prove reliability early.
  • Integration into data, tools, and workflows with guardrails and observability.
  • Ongoing evaluation, audits, and governance reviews to keep systems trusted.

Working cadence

Weekly checkpoints, shared playbooks, and transparent criteria for expanding scope.

Teams know what is in scope, how agents are measured, and when to scale.

Where Archon gets involved

Strategy

Clarify where agents fit, what to automate, and how to measure impact before build decisions are made.

Design & pilots

Translate strategy into scoped pilots with explicit roles, boundaries, and success checkpoints.

Implementation

Integrate into systems with evaluation harnesses, logging, and recovery paths to keep risk low.

Operations

Establish governance rituals, runbooks, and audits so agents remain predictable as they scale.

Engagement models

Discovery sprint

1–2 weeks to map roles, constraints, and measurable objectives. Outputs: role canvas, risk map, pilot plan.

Pilot build

4–6 weeks to deliver a scoped agent with evaluation and observability. Outputs: pilot agent, dashboard, runbook.

Scale-up

Progressive expansion with governance gates and simulation where needed. Outputs: integration plan, escalation playbooks.

Ongoing stewardship

Monthly audits, tuning, and change management to keep agents aligned with business rules.
