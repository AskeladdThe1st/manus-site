What Archon is

An AI firm focused on specialized agents. We ship agents, the frameworks that power them, and the playbooks to run them safely.

Why AI agents matter

Agents turn AI into clear roles: tutors, interviewers, reviewers. With defined scope, you can measure outcomes and earn trust from teams.

What makes Archon different

We design for governance from day one. Every agent ships with constraints, evaluation loops, and integration paths instead of ad-hoc prompts.

Why Specialized Agents?

Intelligence scales better when responsibility is narrow. Specialized agents have a clear role, operate within defined constraints, and are easier to evaluate, improve, and trust.

Generalist systems try to do everything; they become unpredictable, harder to govern, and break accountability. Clarity beats cleverness in production systems.
